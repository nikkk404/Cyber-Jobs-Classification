Defensive Roles

Cybersecurity Analyst

Levels: Entry (0-2 years), Mid (3-5 years), Senior (5+ years)

Description: Monitors and protects systems from cyber threats, investigates breaches, and implements security measures. Daily tasks include log analysis, vulnerability scanning. Skills: Firewalls, encryption, Python. Tools: Wireshark, Splunk. Outlook: High demand, salary 5-30 LPA.

Education & Certifications: BS in Computer Science, CompTIA Security+, CISSP.

SOC Analyst

Levels: Entry (0-2 years), Mid (3-5 years), Senior (5+ years)

Description: Operates in a Security Operations Center to monitor threats in real-time. Tasks include alert triage, incident escalation. Skills: SIEM, network monitoring. Tools: ELK Stack, QRadar. Outlook: Growing demand, salary 4-25 LPA.

Education & Certifications: Associate degree, Security+, GIAC.

Incident Response Specialist

Levels: Entry (0-2 years), Mid (3-5 years), Senior (5+ years)

Description: Responds to security incidents, contains breaches, and recovers systems. Tasks: Forensics, root cause analysis. Skills: Incident handling, communication. Tools: EnCase, FTK. Outlook: Critical role, salary 5.5-40 LPA.

Education & Certifications: BS in IT, GIAC Certified Incident Handler.

Endpoint Security Specialist

Levels: Entry (0-2 years), Mid (3-5 years), Senior (5+ years)

Description: Secures endpoints like laptops and mobiles from threats. Tasks: Deploy EDR, manage policies. Skills: Antivirus, patch management. Tools: CrowdStrike, Microsoft Defender. Outlook: Increasing with remote work, salary 5-30 LPA.

Education & Certifications: BS in Cybersecurity, CompTIA CySA+.

About Cybersecurity

Cybersecurity is the practice of protecting systems, networks, and data from digital attacks. It's crucial in today's world due to increasing cyber threats.

Importance: Protects sensitive information, prevents financial loss, maintains trust.

Growth: The field is expected to grow 32% by 2030, with millions of jobs worldwide. In India, demand is high in IT, finance, govt.

Tips for Entering: Get a degree in CS/IT, earn certs like Security+, gain experience via internships, learn tools like Kali Linux.
